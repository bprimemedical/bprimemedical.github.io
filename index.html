CONSULTATION & EVALUATION

We offer our knowledge and expertise in Medical Treadmills and Cardiac Stress Test Systems. PREVENTATIVE MAINTENANCE & EQUIPMENT REPAIR

Our Biomedical Engineering Technologists are trained and experienced in Preventative Maintenance and Corrective Work for Medical Treadmills and Cardiac Stress Test Systems. Preventative Maintenance can be annual system inspection and/or new system acceptance. Corrective Work is fancy for 'repair'. 

In emergency cases where you need your Medical Treadmills or Cardiac Stress Test Systems accepted or repaired right away, we will do whatever we can to get the job done as fast and as safely as possible.
